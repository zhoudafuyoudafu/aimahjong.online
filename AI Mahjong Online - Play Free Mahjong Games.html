About Mahjong

Mahjong is a tile-based game that originated in China during the Qing dynasty. The game has spread throughout the world since the early 20th century.

The version we offer is Mahjong Solitaire, a single-player matching game that uses a set of mahjong tiles. The goal is to match open pairs of identical tiles and remove them from the board, exposing the tiles under them for play.

Game Rules

Basic Rules

Remove all tiles from the board by matching pairs of identical tiles. A tile is considered "open" or "free" when its left or right side is exposed (not blocked by another tile).

Matching Tiles

Match identical tiles to remove them from the board. Some special tiles like flowers and seasons can be matched with others in their group.

Strategy

Focus on removing tiles that are blocking the most other tiles. Sometimes it's better to leave easy matches for later.
